Welcome to thoughtfull

Organise your thoughts and calm your mind now; download thoughtfull today.

Store Current Thoughts

Add thoughts with ease and choose to lock them away, ready to refelect on at a future date. A reminder can be sent at the chosen end date reminding you to take note of that thought if you wish.

Evaluate and Reflect

When the set date for a given thought is reaces, you are enabled to evaluate and reflect on it, choosing either to archive it for future reference, or remove it if you feel you have conquered that thought.

Share Public Thoughts

There is a public thought section where users are enabled to share their thoughts anonomously with one another; here you can see you are not alone and others are likely experiencing siimilar things to you.

Customise and be Grateful

Change your name and profile picture to make your profile your own! Furthremore, use the profile screen to store aspects of your life you are grateful for each day by typing in the text box and submitting. This data will then be visable on the gratitudes screen.

Usability Overview

Thoughtful will enable users to store thoughts that may be causing them anxiety or stress, as well as other thoughts that may just be useful to remember at a later date. Initially, the application will enable the users to input their desired thought and details of it, i.e., how they are feeling and the reasons for that, and an end date where they can later be reminded of this thought if they choose so; the entered thought will be locked until the timer runs out.


Login and Register

Users are enabled to login to the application once they have registered an account with firebase. An account can be created by pressing the rgister button and filling out the details of the form with the required information - user email, full name and password. These will the be stored and allow the user to login by filling out their username and password on the login page.


Add a problem

Press the add button in the top app bar of the screen; this will take you to a form where you can create a thought by entering the required information: Title, description, end time, end date, specify private or public, whether you would like a reminder or not, and finally whether to lock the post or not.


Search

Press the search button in the top app bar of the screen; this will allow you to filter posts for whichever screen you are on by string based on their title and description.


Screens and navigation

The app can be navigated by the bottom navigation bar, as well as various buttons eithin each screen. The current thoughts page displays currently active posts where their endtime has not been reached. Each post on the current thoughts page can be shared, deleted or archived using the buttons at the bottom of each post. The archive page presents past thoughts once the expiry has been reached, allowing users to decide whether to review, delete or share past thoughts.

The public posts page is a place for users to view public thoughts created by other users, this option is toggled when created a post.

The profile page is used to customse the users profile by profile image and name. This page also provides functionality for users to enter aspects of their life they are grateful for; this information is then presented on the view gratitudes page accessed via the button at the botto of the screen.


Thought Expiry

Upon expiry, a given thought post will be moved to the archive and the user will receive a notification that a thought post has expired. When they are reminded of their thought, users will be given three choices; they may either delete the thought without review if they feel it is not important anymore, view the thought immediately and then decide whether to delete it, or they may decide to leave the thought archived without review, ready to be viewed at a later date if they choose.
